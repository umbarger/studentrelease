Frequently Asked Questions

1. What happens if I forgot my QR tag at home?

Our software has the ability to manually input your QR code number so we can keep the carpool line moving

2. What do I do if my family wants to add a new vehicle, how will I update my information?

New vehicles can be updated via our website under the "Parents" tab.

3. Is it one QR tag per family?

It is one QR tag per vehicle.

4. Can I register multiple vehicles?

Yes. Each vehicle will have its own seperate QR tag registered to it.

5. What do I do if I lose my QR tag?

A new QR tag can be emailed to you! Just email us under the Contact page to get in touch with us!
